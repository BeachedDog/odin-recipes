lasagna

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

    Ingredients
  • pasta sauce
  • noodles
  • cheese
  • spices
  • meats
    Directions
  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles).
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times.
